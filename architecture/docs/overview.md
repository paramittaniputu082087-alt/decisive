[18.43, 8/3/2026] Paramitta Niputu: # Decisive System Architecture

Decisive acts as a decision intelligence layer between decision sources and execution systems.

Main Components

Decision Input Layer  
Receives decision requests from humans, AI systems, or workflows.

Decision Model Engine  
Structures the decision context and available options.

Evaluation & Scoring Layer  
Evaluates options using defined criteria.

Policy & Governance Engine  
Applies organisational policies and risk rules.

Decision Outcome Layer  
Determines whether the decision is approved, escalated, or blocked.

Decision Ledger  
Stores audit records and decision outcomes.
[18.49, 8/3/2026] Paramitta Niputu: # Decisive Overview

Decisive is a decision intelligence framework designed to improve governance and accountability in organisational decision-making.

The system models decisions explicitly, evaluates options using structured criteria, and records outcomes to support transparency and learning.

## Objectives

- Improve decision transparency
- Support structured decision-making
- Enable governance and accountability
- Provide traceable decision outcomes

## Core Idea

Instead of informal discussions or fragmented approvals, Decisive treats decisions as structured systems that can be analysed, reviewed, and improved over time.
